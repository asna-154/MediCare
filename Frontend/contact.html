Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a prescription?

You can upload your prescription by clicking the "Upload Prescription" button on our homepage or in your account dashboard. Simply take a clear photo of your prescription and upload it. Our pharmacists will review it within 1-2 hours during business hours.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Express delivery is available for next-day delivery in most metropolitan areas. Delivery times may vary based on your location and prescription verification requirements.

Are your medicines authentic?

Yes, all medicines sold through MediCare are 100% authentic and sourced directly from licensed manufacturers and distributors. We maintain strict quality control and verification processes to ensure medication safety.

Can I consult with a pharmacist?

Absolutely! Our licensed pharmacists are available 24/7 for consultations. You can chat with them through our website, mobile app, or call our pharmacist helpline. Consultations are free for registered customers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. We also offer cash on delivery in select areas. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

Do you deliver to my area?

We deliver nationwide across the United States. Enter your zip code during checkout to check specific delivery options for your area. We're continuously expanding our delivery network to serve more communities.
